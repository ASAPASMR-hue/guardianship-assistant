Beta Test Preview - Genesee County, Michigan

About This Assistant

This AI assistant provides general information about minor guardianship procedures in Genesee County, Michigan. It can help you understand:

  • Types of guardianship (Full, Limited, Temporary)
  • Filing procedures and required forms
  • Court fees and processes
  • Legal requirements under Michigan law

Important: This tool provides general legal information only, not legal advice. For advice specific to your situation, please consult a licensed Michigan attorney.
